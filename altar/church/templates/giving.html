Your Giving Makes a Difference

At Alter of His Presence Ministry, we believe that giving is an act of worship and a way to express our gratitude to God for all His blessings. Your generous gifts enable us to continue our mission of spreading the gospel, serving our community, and making a positive impact in the lives of others.

We invite you to partner with us in ministry by giving your tithes and offerings. Your financial support helps us to:

  • Support our pastors and staff
  • Maintain and improve our church facilities
  • Fund outreach programs and community events
  • Support missionaries and ministries around the world

Every gift, no matter how small, makes a difference. Thank you for your faithfulness and generosity.

Ways to Give

  • Online Giving
    You can give online using your credit card or bank account. It's quick, easy, and secure. Give Online
  • Text-to-Give
    Text the word "GIVE" to [phone number] to give using your mobile phone.
  • Cash or Check
    You can also give cash or check during our Sunday services or by mailing your gift to the church office.
  • Stock Gifts
    If you would like to give a gift of stock, please contact the church office for instructions.

The Impact of Your Giving

Your giving makes a real difference in the lives of others. Here are just a few examples of how your gifts are used to make a positive impact:

  • We were able to purchase a new van to transport our youth group to mission trips and other events.
  • We were able to provide financial assistance to families in need.
  • We were able to support a local homeless shelter with food and clothing donations.
  • We were able to send a team of missionaries to a remote village in Africa to share the gospel and provide medical care.

Thank you for your generosity and for making a difference in the world.
